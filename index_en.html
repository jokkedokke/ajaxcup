Ajax cup 2016

Invitational tournament for boys born 2004

11.-12.6. 2016 Kempele, Finland

Ajax cup 2016

The third annual Ajax Cup will be held in June 2016 in Kempele, Finland (near the city of Oulu) by the local team Ajax Sarkkiranta.

The invitational tournament for boys born in 2004 will be hosting top quality teams from Finland.

The games will be played in three levels: Premiership, Championship and First Division. The goal is to offer even games for all the participating teams by dividing the tournament into skill categories.

Participation fee will be 270€ for Permiership and Championship, and 170€ for the First Division.

The last two Premiership titles have been claimed by juniors of the Veikkausliiga team Kuopion Palloseura (KuPS). In 2014 the title was given after a penalty shootout versus SJK, and in 2015 KuPS defeated the local team OLS in the final. Who will claim the title in 2016?

Tournament info

Fixtures

All games will be 8 vs8. The winner of each skill category will be awarded with a trophy, and the best goal scorer and best goalkeeper of every category will receive a special award.

Pitches

The games will be played on two natural grass pitches (63x45m) and on two artificial pitches (66x47m).

Game time

Game time will be 2x15mins per game, or 2x20mins, depending on the number of participants. Each team will have at least 6 games during the two tournament days.

Lunches and accommodation

The teams will have a chance to have lunch near a school in the near vinicity of the pitches. The hotel services of the city of Oulu are just 15 minutes away. Special hotel packages will be available for the teams.

More information regarding lunches and the options for accommodation will be given later on.

WBA-leiri

Football Development Schools (in association with West Bromwich Albion) will arrange a football camp in the week following the tournament.

The coaches of FDS-WBA and Ajax-Sarkkiranta will be in charge of the camp. A player scour of the WBA will also be present on the site.

100 junior players will be selected for the camp, which will be arranged on the 14.-17. of June. The participants will be from age groups girls born in 2001-03, and boys born in 2002-06.

More information and enrollment through the Ajax-Sarkkiranta website..
