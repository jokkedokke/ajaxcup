17.-18.6. 2017 Kempele, Finland

Ajax cup 2017

The fourth annual Ajax Cup will be held in June 2017 in Kempele, Finland (near the city of Oulu) by the local team Ajax Sarkkiranta.

The invitational tournament for boys born in 2004 will be hosting top quality teams from Finland.

The games will be played in two levels: Competition 1 (11v11) and competition 2 (8v8).

Participation fee will be 300€ for Competition 1, and 200€ for the competition 2.

The first two Ajax cup titles have been claimed by juniors of the Veikkausliiga team Kuopion Palloseura (KuPS). In 2016 the title was clinched by JJK Jyväskylä, after beating the host team Ajax-Sarkkiranta in the finals.

Tournament info

Fixtures

The games will be played 11v11 or 8v8. The winner of both series will be awarded with a trophy, and the best goal scorer and best goalkeeper of both categories will receive a special award.

Pitches

The games will be played on two natural grass pitches (63x45m) and on two artificial pitches (66x47m).

Game time

Game time will be 2x20 mins in both groups.

Lunches and accommodation

The teams will have a chance to have lunch near a school in the near vinicity of the pitches. The hotel services of the city of Oulu are just 15 minutes away. Special hotel packages will be available for the teams.

More information regarding lunches and the options for accommodation will be given later on.
